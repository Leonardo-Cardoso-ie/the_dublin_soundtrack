The Dublin Soundtrack

Join us


We are looking for the perfect beat! The harmony between chords, lyrics, we want to hear Dublin music

In partnership with Gisela Records (the label that released Mano Scoob, Us bayoneta, One Leo band and many others) to make their studios available for us to record the Dublin Soundtrack, a collection of 21 original songs, which will be widely publicized in the media and a launch event, to sign up, you don't have to pay anything! It's completely free and you don't have to be a professional musician, and if your song is selected, you will have a single of your own, recorded by the Gisela records label.

Sign up, send your audio or video (in good quality and a maximum of 6 minutes) or deliver your material to our address

cross your fingers and have faith, if you have been selected, we will get in touch with you
