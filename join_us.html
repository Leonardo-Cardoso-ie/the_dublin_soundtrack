The Dublin Soundtrack

Join us


We are looking for the perfect beat! The harmony between chords, lyrics, we want to hear Dublin music.

The partnership with Gisela Records allowed us to use their studio and record the Dublin Soundtrack to make their studios available for us to record the Dublin Soundtrack. The project is a collection of 21 original songs, which will be widely publicized in the media and a launch event. To sign up, you don't have to pay anything! It's completely free. You don't have to be a professional musician. If your song is selected, you will have a single of your own, recorded by the Gisela records label (the label that released Mano Scoob, Us bayoneta, One Leo band and many others).

Sign up, send your audio or video (in good quality and a maximum of 6 minutes) or deliver your material to our address

Fingers crossed, if you have been selected, we will get in touch with you
